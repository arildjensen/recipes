Apple Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Cream milk, 1 pint/500 mL
  • Coconut milk, 1/2 cup/100 mL
  • 2 chicken bouillon cubes
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 4 chicken fillets (one per person)
  • 1 large apple
  • 1 onion
  • Broccoli florets, 1/2 lb/200 g

Serving size

Serves 4 people

Directions

  1. In a casserolle, melt the butter over medium head and add the flour.
  2. Add cream milk, bouillon cubes, and coconut milk while stirring.
  3. Add curry powder and continue stirring until mixture boils and thickens. Set aside.
  4. Chop the apple and the onion into thin slices.
  5. Make a large slit in each fillet and stuff each piece of chicken with apple and onion slices. Place the chicken into an oven-safe dish.
  6. Add the remaining apple and onion slices into the dish and add the broccoli. Cover with the curry sauce.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes at 375 F/190 C.
  8. Serve with white rice.

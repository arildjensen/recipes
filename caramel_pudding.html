Caramel Pudding

Norwegian-style caramel flan made from scratch. Must be made one day in advance.

You Will Need

  • Pyrex bread pan
  • Larger Pyrex pan
  • Measuring scale, in grams
  • Measuring cup, in milliliters
  • Bowl
  • Sieve
  • Medium skillet
  • Boiling water

Shopping List

Item Amount
Sugar 1 lb 300 g
Whole Milk 1 quart 1 L
Cream Milk 1 pint 0.5 L
Eggs 8 ct.
Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Set oven to 390 °F 200 °C
  2. Melt 200 g sugar over low heat in a medium skillet. Do not stir until melted. When dark brown pour into Pyrex bread pan, try to coat the sides.
  3. Heat up 400 mL cream milk, 100 g sugar, and 800 mL whole milk.
  4. Add 1 tbsp vanilla extract.
  5. Let mix cool off until warm to the touch.
  6. In bowl gently mix eggs together.
  7. Using a sieve add eggs to mix.
  8. Create a waterbath by placing the Pyrex bread pan inside a larger Pyrex pan, add boiling water until 3/4 full.
  9. Place inside oven and lower the temperature to 260 °F 125 °C.
  10. After 2 hours when the mix is firm to the touch (use a wet spoon), let it cool off and place in a refrigerator until the next day.
  11. To serve first carefully cut the sides of the bread pan, then place a large plate on top of bread form (upside down), then flip and the pudding will pop out of the pan.

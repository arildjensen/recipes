Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream, 1 pint/500 mL
  • Egg yolks, 4
  • Sugar, 3 tbsp
  • Vanilla, 1 tsp
  • Salt, 1 pinch
  • Chopped dark chocolate, 7 oz/200 g

Serving size

Serves 8 people

Directions

  1. Heat 3/4 cup cream in one-quart heavy saucepan until hot
  2. Whisk together yolks, sugar, and salt in metal bowl
  3. Add hot cream into metal bown while whisking
  4. Transfer mixture to saucepan
  5. Heat until 160°F/70°C using moderately low heat, stirring constantly
  6. Pour custardthrough fine-mesh sieve into bown and stir in vanilla
  7. Melt chocolate in microwave
  8. Mix chocolate with custard
  9. Beat remaining 1 1/4 cups cream in bowl with electric mixer until it just holds stiff peaks
  10. Whisk one-fourth of cream into chocolate custard to lighten, then fold in remaining cream gently but thoroughly
  11. Spoon mousse into 8 gasses or ramekins and chill, covered, at least 6 hours. Let stand at room temperature about 20 minutes before serving.

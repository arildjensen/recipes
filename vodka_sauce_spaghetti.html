Vodka Sauce Spaghetti

Simple but good vodka sauce spaghetti meal for 2-4 people.

You Will Need

  • About 1.5 hours of your time
  • Deep frying pan
  • Regular frying pan
  • Medium pot
  • Cutting board

Shopping List

Item Amount
Ground meat 1 lb 500 g
Yellow onion Small
Garlic Tube paste or 2 cloves
Can of crushed tomatoes 28 oz 800 g
Heavy cream 1/2 pint 250 mL
Vodka 200 mL
Fresh basil 1/2 oz 15 g
Spaghetti pasta

Instructions

  1. Chop the onions into small pieces
  2. Coarsely chop the garlic if not using paste
  3. In the deep pan, fry the onions and garlic until translucent (do NOT overcook)
  4. Add can of crushed tomatoes
  5. Add vodka, about 3/4 cup
  6. Cook until the alcohol is gone, about 20-30 minutes
  7. While waiting, fry the ground beef in the regular pan
  8. Finely chop the basil
  9. Add the heavy cream, cooked meat, and basil
  10. Let simmer for 20-30 minutes
  11. Cook the spaghetti pasta: bring water to boil, add pasta, wait 14 minutes while occasionally stirring
